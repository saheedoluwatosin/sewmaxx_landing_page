About Sewmaxx

Sewmaxx is an online market place that connects independent tailors with real clients via Telegram and Whatsapp. Whether you're based in Nigeria, Accra or any African country, clients can now find you faster

Charges & Commissions

To keep Sewmaxx running smoothly and reward affiliates,

Direct Client Orders

Tailors keep 85-90% of the order

Affiliate Client Orders

Tailors keep 60% of the order
