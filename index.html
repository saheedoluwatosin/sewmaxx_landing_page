Frequently Asked Questions

Here's everything you need to know about being listed
as Tailors on Sewmaxx

Do I need to pay to join Sewmaxx?

No, joining Sewmaxx is completely free. You only pay commission fees when you receive and complete orders through the platform.

Do I need a physical shop?

No, you don't need a physical shop. You can work from home as long as you have a verified workshop space and can deliver quality services.

How do clients discover me?

Clients find you through the Sewmaxx platform by searching based on location, specialty, and budget range that matches your profile.

How do I get paid for my work?

Payments are processed through the platform after order completion and client confirmation. You receive your earnings directly to your registered account.

What happens if a client is not satisfied?

Sewmaxx has a dispute resolution process. We work with both parties to find a fair solution while maintaining quality standards.

Are there benefits apart from client orders?

Yes! You can earn through referral programs, receive promotional vouchers, and benefit from increased visibility in your area.

Can I update my profile after joining?

Absolutely! You can update your services, portfolio, pricing, and availability anytime through your dashboard.
